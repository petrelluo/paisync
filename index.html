About Us

Built for performance marketers

PAISync is an AI-powered advertising optimization platform headquartered in Milpitas, California.

PAISync, Inc. is a B2B SaaS company that builds autonomous AI agents for digital advertising professionals. Our platform connects to the world's leading advertising networks — including Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Amazon DSP — and uses machine learning to continuously optimize campaign performance on behalf of our customers.

We were founded with a single mission: eliminate the manual, repetitive work of managing paid media so that marketing teams can focus on strategy, creativity, and growth. PAISync's AI engine monitors performance signals 24/7, autonomously reallocating budgets, adjusting bids, and testing creatives to maximize return on ad spend (ROAS).

Our customers range from direct-to-consumer brands and e-commerce operators to full-service digital agencies managing portfolios of client accounts. PAISync is built for teams that need to move fast, optimize continuously, and report clearly — without hiring a dedicated ops team for every platform.

We operate under the principles of transparency, security, and customer control. Advertisers retain full ownership of their campaign data, and our platform accesses ad platform APIs exclusively through OAuth 2.0 — meaning we never store passwords or access credentials.

Our Mission
To make enterprise-grade advertising automation accessible to every performance marketing team — so great products can reach the audiences that need them, efficiently and at scale.

What We Build
An autonomous AI optimization layer that sits above your existing ad accounts. PAISync reads performance data via official platform APIs, identifies opportunities in real time, and executes changes — budget shifts, bid adjustments, creative rotations — within the guardrails you define.
🔒
How We Handle Data
We process advertising performance data strictly on behalf of our customers. We do not sell data, do not use it for cross-customer targeting, and maintain industry-standard security controls including TLS 1.3 encryption, AES-256 data at rest, and OAuth 2.0 API access. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
📍
Where We Operate
PAISync is incorporated in the United States and serves customers globally. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with primary operations in the US. We comply with applicable data protection regulations including CCPA and GDPR.
🔗
How PAISync uses advertising platform APIs

PAISync connects to Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Amazon DSP exclusively through their official OAuth 2.0 APIs. We access your ad account data solely to deliver campaign management and optimization services on your behalf. We do not sell your data, do not use platform API data to train AI models, and do not share your campaign data with any third party except as required to operate the service. PAISync's use of Google API data adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
